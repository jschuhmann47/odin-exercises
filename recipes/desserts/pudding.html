Argentinian Pudding

Description

This is a traditional Argentinian dessert that goes well with cream or dulce de leche, also called flan

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 500cc of milk
  • 200g of sugar

Steps

  1. Break the 5 eggs in a bowl and add 100g of sugar. Beat the contents lightly to disperse the eggs
  2. Add the milk and beat until it gets an uniform texture
  3. Put the other 100g of sugar in a pudding pan with moderate fire. Keep dispersing it unitl caramel is made.
  4. When the caramel is ready, drop the bowl contents in the pan.
  5. Grab a big metallic recipent and add some water. Put the pan inside and put it in the oven.
  6. Heat the oven at 160Â°C for 40 minutes
  7. Done!
